# Topics Research

## Question
How does Daniel 8 connect to Daniel 9? Trace six lines of evidence. How do chazon and mar'eh track independently?

## Topics

### GABRIEL
- (A messenger of God)
- Appeared to:
  - Daniel: DAN 8:16; 9:21
  - Zacharias: LUK 1:11-19
  - Mary: LUK 1:26-29
- **Note:** Gabriel appears by name exactly FOUR times in all of Scripture. The two OT occurrences are both in Daniel (8:16 and 9:21). He is completely absent from Daniel 2, 7, 10-12 by name.

### DANIEL
- A Jewish captive, also called BELTESHAZZAR
- Educated at king's court (DAN 1)
- Interprets visions (DAN 2; 4; 5)
- Promotion and executive authority (DAN 2:48,49; 5:11,29; 6:2)
- Conspiracy against, cast into the lions' den (DAN 6)
- Prophecies of (DAN 4:8,9; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12; MAT 24:15)
- Abstinence of (DAN 1:8-16)
- Wisdom of (DAN 1:17; EZK 28:3)
- Devoutness of (DAN 2:18; 6; 9; 10; 12; EZK 14:14)
- Courage and fidelity of (DAN 4:27; 5:17-23; 6:10-23)

### SEVENTY
- The senate of the Israelites composed of seventy elders (EXO 24:1,9; NUM 11:16,24,25)
- The seventy disciples sent forth by Jesus (LUK 10:1-17)
- The Jews in captivity in Babylon for seventy years (JER 25:11,12; 29:10; DAN 9:2; ZEC 1:12; 7:5)
- 70 Weeks in the vision of Daniel (DAN 9:24)

### VISION (from Nave's)
- NUM 12:6; 1SA 3:1; 2CH 26:5; PSA 89:19; PRO 29:18
- JER 14:14; 23:16; DAN 1:17; HOS 12:10; JOL 2:28; HAB 2:2; ACT 2:17
- Daniel's visions: DAN 7 (four beasts); 7:9-27 (Ancient of Days); 8 (ram and he-goat); 10 (the angel)

### PROPHECY
- ISA 28:22; LUK 1:70; 2TI 3:16; 2PE 1:21
- DAN 9:2; 9:24; 9:26,27; DAN 7:14,27

### DAILY OFFERING
- EXO 29:38-42; 30:7-9; NUM 28:3-8; EZR 3:4-6; EZK 46:13-15
- DAN 9:21,26,27; 11:31; ACT 3:1
- **Note:** Dan 9:21's "evening oblation" (minchath ereb) is the timing marker for Gabriel's return — connecting his appearance to the sanctuary system under attack in Dan 8.

### PRAYER (Daniel instances)
- DAN 2:19-23 (interpretation of dream)
- DAN 9:3,16-19 (intercession for the people)
- DAN 9:20-23 (Gabriel appears during prayer)
- DAN 10:12 ("set thine heart to understand")

### FASTING (Daniel instances)
- DAN 9:3 (with prayer for deliverance)
- DAN 10:1-3 (three-week fast)
- DAN 6:18 (Darius fasts)

### CAPTIVITY (Judah — prophetic framework)
- ISA 39:6; JER 13:19; 20:4; 25:2-11; 32:28
- 2KI 24:11-16; 25; 2CH 36; JER 52:28-30
- EZR 1; 2; 3; 8 (return from captivity)
- JER 25:11,12; 29:10 (70-year prophecy)

## Related Topics Discovered

### MESSENGER
- HAG 1:13; MAL 2:7; 3:1; 4:5,6; MAT 11:10; MRK 1:2; LUK 7:27; 2CO 12:7
- Gabriel functions as God's designated messenger for prophetic timing revelations.

### ANGEL
- DAN 3:25,28; 4:13-17; 6:22; 10:6; GEN 28:12; LUK 1:11-20,26; ACT 27:23
- The angel in Dan 10-12 is described but NOT identified as Gabriel by name.

### BOOK
- GEN 5:1; EXO 32:32,33; PSA 69:28; 87:6; DAN 12:1; PSA 56:8; 139:16; MAL 3:16
- Daniel "understood by books" (Dan 9:2) — sepher as a mode of revelation distinct from chazon.
