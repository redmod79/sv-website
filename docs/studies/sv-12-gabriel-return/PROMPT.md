# Bible Study: Gabriel's Return — Daniel 8 Connects to Daniel 9

## Question
How does Daniel 8 connect to Daniel 9? Trace six independent lines of evidence: (1) Gabriel's back-reference in 9:21, (2) identical commission formula (8:16 = 9:23), (3) failure-resolution arc (8:27 to 10:1), (4) 'many days' echo (8:26 = 10:14), (5) shared vocabulary network, (6) Daniel's study of Jeremiah in 9:2. How do chazon and mar'eh track independently?
