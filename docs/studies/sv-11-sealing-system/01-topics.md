# Topics Research

## Question
What do satham and chatham mean? What four mechanisms unseal the vision? Trace the vocabulary chain from Dan 8:26 through Rev 22:10. What was actually sealed — apply four criteria.

## Topics

### SEAL
**Source:** Nave's Topical Bible (`naves_db.py --topic "SEAL"`)

**1. A stamp used for signifying documents**
- Given as a pledge: GEN 38:18
- Engraved: EXO 28:11,21,36; 39:6,14,30; 2TI 2:19
- Decrees signified by: 1KI 21:8; EST 8:8
- Documents sealed with:
  - Ahab's letter: 1KI 21:8
  - Covenants: NEH 9:38; 10:1; ISA 8:16
  - Decrees: EST 8:8; DAN 6:9
  - Deeds: JER 32:10
- Treasures secured by: DEU 32:34
- Lion's den secured by: DAN 6:17
- The gravesite of Jesus: MAT 27:66
- Circumcision was regarded as a seal of righteousness: ROM 4:11

**FIGURATIVE**
- Of secrecy: DAN 12:9; REV 5:1
- Of certainty of divine approval: JHN 6:27; ROM 15:28; 2CO 1:22; EPH 1:13; 4:30; REV 7:3,4

### PROPHECY (excerpted — sealing/fulfillment sections)
**Source:** Nave's Topical Bible (`naves_db.py --topic "PROPHECY"`)

- Inspired: ISA 28:22; LUK 1:70; 2TI 3:16; 2PE 1:21
- Written in books: JER 45:1; 51:60
- Proof of God's foreknowledge: ISA 43:9
- Sure fulfillment of: EZK 12:22-25,28; HAB 2:3; MAT 5:18; 24:35; ACT 13:27,29

Key verses for this study: The fulfillment-certainty passages (Hab 2:3, Mat 5:18, Ezk 12:22-25) connect to the authentication sense of chatham. The cessation reference (Lam 2:9) is the only OT text asserting prophecy stopping — relevant to the Dan 9:24 "seal up vision and prophet" discussion.

## Related Topics Discovered

### REVELATION (Nave's)
- The concept of divine disclosure overlaps with unsealing. Key distinction: apokalypsis (G602) is the act of revealing; sphragizo (G4972) is the act of concealing. Rev 22:10 commands non-sealing — the revelation stands open.

### DANIEL (Nave's)
- DAN 1:17 — God gave Daniel understanding in all visions and dreams (beginning of the biyn chain)
- EZK 14:14; 28:3 — Daniel's reputation for wisdom ("there is no secret that they can hide from thee")
- DAN 2; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12 — the full prophetic corpus that constitutes what is sealed
