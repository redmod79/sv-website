# Bible Study: The Seventy Weeks Prophecy — Starting Point, Day-Year Principle, Calculation, and Verification Key

## Question
What is the 70 weeks prophecy? Starting point? Day-year principle (Num 14:34, Ezek 4:6)? Calculate 457 BC + 483 = AD 27. 70th week events? How do 70 weeks verify the 2300?

### Focus Areas

1. **The chathak-link: 70 weeks "cut off" from the 2300.** Tool discoveries confirm H2852 chathak is a hapax legomenon in Dan 9:24. The grammar reference (dan-9-24-27.md) parses it as Niphal.Perf.3ms — a passive "severed/cut off." The research agent should READ `H2852-chathak.md`, then verify the hapax status and its Akkadian cognate background. This is the foundational link establishing that the 70 weeks share a starting point with the 2300.

2. **The day-year principle: declaration vs. application.** Nave's DAY entry explicitly lists "Prophetic" day-texts: DAN 8:14; 9:24-27; 12:11,12; REV 11:3; 12:6; 2PE 3:8. The grammar reference library has `day-year-formula.md`, `distributive-lamed-ratio.md`, and `apocalyptic-time-unit-grammar.md`. The research agent should READ all three grammar entries, retrieve NUM 14:34 and EZK 4:4-6 with full chapter context, and run hebrew_parser.py on both to verify the yom lashshanah construction.

3. **The starting point: which decree?** ARTAXERXES entry lists EZR 7; NEH 2; 5:14. JERUSALEM entry adds EZR 1:1-4; 4:7-24; 6:1-14. Ezra 6:14 is critical — it may present all three decrees as one composite "commandment." The research agent should retrieve EZR 1:1-4, EZR 6:14, EZR 7:11-26, and NEH 2:1-8 with full chapter context. Run parallels on EZR 7:25-26 to find cross-references. Only Artaxerxes' decree in Ezra 7 matches "restore AND build."

4. **The sabbatic-year background: 490 = 70 x 7 missed sabbatical years.** SABBATIC YEAR entry lists LEV 25; LEV 26:34,35; 2CH 36:21. SEVENTY entry lists JER 25:11,12; 29:10; DAN 9:2. The 70-year exile compensated for 490 missed sabbatical years — Gabriel's response of 70 weeks (= 490 years) mirrors this exact number. The research agent should retrieve LEV 26:34-35, 2CH 36:21, and JER 25:11-12 to establish this parallel. READ grammar reference `jer-25-11-12-seventy-years.md`.

5. **The six infinitives of Dan 9:24 and their DOA vocabulary.** The grammar reference (dan-9-24-27.md) parses six purpose infinitives: lekhalle (to finish), ulehathem (to seal up sins), ulekapper (to atone), ulehabi (to bring in), velachtom (to seal), velimshoch (to anoint). Three are sin-removal (pesha, chattaah, avon); three are positive establishment (tsedeq olamim, chatham chazon, mashach qodesh qodashim). The research agent should READ the word studies for all six key terms (H3615, H6588, H2403, H3722, H5771, H6663, H5769, H2856, H2377, H4886, H6944) and map each infinitive to its DOA parallel in Lev 16.

6. **The calculation: 457 BC + 483 = AD 27 verification.** MRK 1:15 uses pleroo + kairos: "The time (ho kairos) is fulfilled (peplērotai)." GAL 4:4 uses pleroma + chronos: "the fulness of the time." The research agent should retrieve MRK 1:15, GAL 4:4, LUK 3:1-3, and run greek_parser.py on MRK 1:15 and GAL 4:4 to verify the verb forms. READ `G4137-pleroo.md` and `G2540-kairos.md`. Also retrieve LUK 3:1-2 for the six-ruler synchronism that dates Jesus' baptism.

7. **The 70th week events: gabar beriyth vs. karath beriyth.** H1396 gabar means "strengthen/make strong" — it implies strengthening an EXISTING covenant, not cutting a new one (which would use H3772 karath). Dan 9:27's "he shall confirm the covenant with many for one week" has a Messianic sustained subject from vv.25-26. The research agent should READ `H1396-gabar.md` and `H3772-karath.md`, then run search_strongs.py --verses H1396 to find all gabar contexts. Also retrieve ISA 42:6, ISA 49:8 (covenant language about Messiah), and DAN 9:27 with full context.

8. **The midst of the week: sacrifice and oblation cease.** Dan 9:27 "in the midst of the week he shall cause the sacrifice (zebach) and the oblation (minchah) to cease (shabath Hiphil)." This typologically terminates the tamid system. The research agent should READ `H4503-minchah.md` and `H8548-tamid.md`, retrieve MAT 27:50-51 (veil rent), HEB 10:11-12 (standing/sitting contrast), COL 2:14 (nailing to cross), and run parallels on DAN 9:27.

9. **The verification key function: testable subset authenticates the parent period.** This is the series' distinctive contribution.
