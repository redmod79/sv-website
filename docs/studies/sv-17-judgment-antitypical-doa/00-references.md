# Reference Gathering: Daniel 7:9-10 as the Antitypical Day of Atonement

## Question
How does Daniel 7:9-10 (thrones placed, Ancient of Days, books opened) correspond to Leviticus 16? Test five thematic parallels: white garments, fire, cloud/incense, judicial examination, exclusion. Does the parallel hold structurally or lexically? What do the books contain? How does the kaphar-to-tsadaq progression provide the linguistic bridge?

## Study Plan Context

The study plan (FRESH-DANIEL-STUDY-PLAN-v3.md) places this study within the Daniel 8:14 series as Study 17 of 23. Key plan-level notes:

- **DOA structural parallels to judgment scene** (5-element correspondence): White garments (Lev 16:4 bad / Dan 7:9 lebus chivar); fire (Lev 16:12 / Dan 7:9-10); cloud (Lev 16:2 / Dan 7:13 anan H6050 hapax); judicial examination (Lev 16:16 / Dan 7:10 books opened); exclusion during process (Lev 16:17 / Rev 15:8)
- **Linen/white garment chain** spans both testaments: bad (H906) Lev 16:4 → Ezek 9:2 → Dan 10:5 → Dan 7:9; leukos (G3022) Rev 1:14, 4:4, 20:11
- **Three-phase judgment structure** resolves Dan 7 / Rev 20 timing: Phase A pre-advent (Dan 7:9-10; Rev 14:7); Phase B millennial review (Rev 20:4; 1 Cor 6:2-3); Phase C post-millennial execution (Rev 20:11-15)
- **kaphar → tsadaq progression**: Lev 16:30 (kaphar → taher), Dan 9:24 (lekhapper → tsedeq olamim), Isa 53:11 (bearing iniquities → yatsdiq), Rom 3:25-26 (hilasterion → dikaios)
- **Dan 7 EXPANDS Dan 2** by inserting the judgment mechanism: Dan 2 goes metals → stone → kingdom; Dan 7 goes beasts → horn → JUDGMENT → kingdom

**Series Insights Document** (dan-8-14-series-insights.md) flags three primary insights for this study:
- **Insight #5** (structural not lexical): The parallel holds at structural level, not through direct lexical dependence. Five thematic parallels exist but through different vocabulary at each transition. Cloud is weakest. Books contain "works" (Rev 20:12), broader than tamid-transferred sins.
- **Insight #4** (kaphar→tsadaq): The strongest linguistic bridge: Lev 16:30 (kaphar→taher) → Dan 9:24 (kaphar→tsedeq) → Isa 53:11 (bearing→yatsdiq) → Rom 3:25-26 (hilasterion→dikaios).
- **Insight #21** (DOA chiastic structure): The DOA's inward→pivot→outward structure mirrors the judgment's architectural logic.
- Secondary: **Insight #18** (three-phase accomplishment — cross, intercession, judgment).
