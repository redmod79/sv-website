### Distribution Verification: tsadaq in Sanctuary/Ritual Contexts

**Claim to verify (Bohr):** tsadaq is "not connected with the ritual of the sanctuary but rather is a legal term."

**Finding from H6663-tsadaq.md distribution table:**
- Job: 23 occurrences (56%)
- Isaiah: 8 (20%)
- Psalms: 5
- Daniel: 4 (including Dan 8:14 nitsdaq, Dan 9:24 via context, Dan 12:3 matsdiqey)
- Genesis: 3
- Ezekiel: 3
- Exodus: 2 (Exo 23:7 courtroom context — "I will not justify the wicked")
- Deuteronomy: 1 (Deu 25:1 — courtroom: "justify the righteous, condemn the wicked")

**Leviticus: 0 occurrences.**
**Numbers: 0 occurrences.**

tsadaq does not appear in ANY of the ritual legislation of the Pentateuch. Its two Pentateuchal occurrences (Exo 23:7 and Deu 25:1) are in judicial/courtroom contexts, not ritual/sanctuary contexts. **Bohr's claim is confirmed by the data.**

The vocabulary gap between Leviticus (kaphar/taher vocabulary) and the vindication vocabulary (tsadaq) is absolute. Dan 9:24 is the ONLY verse in Scripture where both vocabulary systems appear together (lekhapper avon + tsedeq olamim).
