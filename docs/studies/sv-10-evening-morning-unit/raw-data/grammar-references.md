### 4. passages/gen-1-5-creation-day-formula.md
Full analysis of creation formula. Key data:
- Formula: vayehi-erev vayehi-boqer yom [numeral] = three syntactic units (two WayX + one NmCl)
- Verbatim identical across all 6 days; ABSENT from day 7
- Day 1 uses cardinal echad (not ordinal rishon)
- Day 6 has article on ordinal (ha-shishi) — climactic marking
- Gen 1:5 vs Dan 8:14: both use same nouns in same order, both absolute state, both without articles — but Genesis is syndetic (separated by vayehi verbs) while Daniel is asyndetic (compressed into compound)

### 5. hebrew/asyndetic-noun-pair.md
Canonical grammar concept. Key data:
- Definition: two nouns juxtaposed without conjunction, forming a single conceptual unit
- Three functions: (1) compound unit, (2) distributive repetition, (3) asyndetic enumeration
- Dan 8:14 is the flagship example of Function 1 (compound time-unit)
- Diagnostic: both nouns Noun.*.Abs, no Conj/Art/Prep between them
- Semantic effects: fusion (one concept, not two), register marking (non-standard unit)
- Contrast with construct chain (genitive), waw-coordination (two separate items), apposition (same referent restated)

### 6. hebrew/stem-niphal.md
Niphal stem reference. Key data:
- Primary functions: simple passive, reflexive, tolerative/middle
- Dan 8:14 nitsdaq = simple passive (subject receives vindication)
- Only Niphal of tsadaq in entire OT
