# Existing Studies — Summaries

## Series Studies (from 00-references.md)

### sv-15-2300-calculation
- Question: How does Daniel 8-9 establish the 2300/70-week relationship?
- Key finding: hapax verb chathak (H2852) proves 70 weeks "cut from" 2300; 457 BC + 2300 = 1844 AD
- DOA calendar correspondence: terminus at 10th of Tishri season
- Rev 14:7 announces this event: "the hour of his judgment is come"
- Chain: Dan 8:14 → Dan 7:9-10 → Rev 14:7

### sv-17-judgment-antitypical-doa
- Question: How does Dan 7:9-10 correspond to Lev 16?
- Key finding: kaphar-to-tsadaq linguistic progression bridges DOA to vindication
- Dan 9:24 is the ONLY verse where DOA vocabulary (kaphar) and vindication vocabulary (tsedeq) appear together
- Three-phase judgment: Phase A pre-advent (Dan 7:9-10; Rev 14:7), Phase B millennial (Rev 20:4), Phase C post-millennial (Rev 20:11-15)

### sv-18-vindication-meaning
- Question: What does nitsdaq qodesh mean?
- Key finding: forensic verdict, not ritual cleansing; four-fold attack answered by four-fold vindication
- Five beneficiaries: people, character, ministry, truth, universe
- Vindication declarations (Rev 15:3, 16:7, 19:1-2) use dikaios + alethinos pair = Greek equivalents of tsedeq + emeth
- Rev 16:7: altar speaks the verdict — closest NT parallel to nitsdaq qodesh
- Rev 19:1-2: both verbs aorist (ekrinen, exedikesen) — vindication completed
- matsdiqey-nitsdaq connection: Dan 12:3 maskilim "turn many to righteousness" (Hiphil of tsadaq)

### sv-20-rev-10-unsealing
- Question: What is the "little book open" in Rev 10?
- Key finding: Daniel's sealed prophecy, now permanently opened
- Rev 10:11 "prophesy again" bridges into the three angels' messages
- Four-fold audience formula uses Danielic vocabulary (Dan 7:14)
- chronos ouketi estai = cessation of prophetic time periods

### sv-09-counterfeit-system
- Question: What does the little horn replace the tamid with?
- Key finding: horn substitutes, not merely removes; four counterfeits mapped
- Dan 2:21/7:25 shna contrast: God changes (participle), horn intends to change (infinitive)
- emeth-dath-torah equation: emeth (Dan 8:12) = dath (Dan 7:25) = torah (Psa 119:142)
- nitsdaq reverses each counterfeit
