# Reference Gathering: Daniel 9:24 — The Six Infinitival Purposes

## Question
Daniel 9:24 lists six infinitival purposes. Three address the problem: finish transgression (pesha), end sins, make reconciliation (kaphar). Three establish the result: bring everlasting righteousness (tsedeq olamim), seal up vision (chatham authentication), anoint most Holy (qodesh qodashim). How does this verse uniquely bridge DOA vocabulary (kaphar) and vindication vocabulary (tsedeq)? How does chatak (hapax) prove the 70 weeks are severed from the 2300?
