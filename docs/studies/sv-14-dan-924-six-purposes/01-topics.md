# Topics Research

## Question
Daniel 9:24's six infinitival purposes. How does this verse bridge DOA vocabulary (kaphar) and vindication vocabulary (tsedeq)? How does chatak prove 70 weeks severed from 2300? Does qodesh qodashim refer to heavenly sanctuary inauguration?
