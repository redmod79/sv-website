# Raw Hebrew/Aramaic Parsing Data

## Daniel 1:4 (biyn chain — capacity)
Hebrew: יְלָדִים אֲשֶׁר אֵין־בָּהֶם כָּל־מוּם וְטוֹבֵי מַרְאֶה וּמַשְׂכִּילִים בְּכָל־חָכְמָה וְיֹדְעֵי דַעַת וּמְבִינֵי מַדָּע

Key words:
- מַשְׂכִּילִים (maskilim) — Verb.Hiphil.Ptcp.mp of שׂכל — "skilful/wise"
- מְבִינֵי (mebiyney) — Verb.Hiphil.Ptcp.mp.Cst of בין — "understanding"
- מַרְאֶה (mar'eh) — Noun.ms.Abs — "sight/appearance" (used for physical appearance here)

Key words:

## Daniel 8:26 (chazon/mar'eh PROOF TEXT)

Key words:

- הָעֶרֶב (ha-erev) — Art + Noun.ms.Abs — "the evening"
- הַבֹּקֶר (ha-boqer) — Art + Noun.ms.Abs — "the morning"
- נֶאֱמַר (ne'emar) — Verb.Niphal.Perf.3ms of אמר — "was told" (passive)
- אֱמֶת (emeth) — Noun.fs.Abs — "truth/trustworthiness"
- סְתֹם (setom) — Verb.Qal.Impv.2ms of סתם — "seal up/shut"
- הֶחָזוֹן (he-chazon) — Art + Noun.ms.Abs — "THE vision" (H2377)

CRITICAL DISTINCTION:
- The MAR'EH of the evening-morning → declared TRUE (authenticated)
- The CHAZON → commanded to be SEALED (deferred)

## Daniel 9:22-23 (biyn chain — RESUMPTION)
v.22: וַיָּבֶן וַיְדַבֵּר עִמִּי וַיֹּאמַר דָּנִיֵּאל עַתָּה יָצָאתִי לְהַשְׂכִּילְךָ בִינָה

Key words:
- וַיָּבֶן (yabiyn) — Verb.Hiphil.Wayq.3ms of בין — "he caused to understand / informed"
- לְהַשְׂכִּילְךָ (lehaskilkha) — Verb.Hiphil.InfCon.+2ms of שׂכל — "to give thee skill"
- בִינָה (biynah) — Noun.fs.Abs — "understanding"

v.23: וּבִין בַּדָּבָר וְהָבֵן בַּמַּרְאֶה

Key words:
- בִין (biyn) — Verb.Qal.Impv.2ms of בין — "understand!" (imperative)
- הָבֵן (habiyn) — Verb.Hiphil.Impv.2ms of בין — "consider/make understand!" (causative imperative)
- הַמַּרְאֶה (ham-mar'eh) — Art + Noun.ms.Abs — "THE mar'eh" (H4758)

CRITICAL: The double imperative in 9:23 pairs biyn (Qal — personal understanding) with habiyn (Hiphil — deeper comprehension) regarding the mar'eh specifically.

## Daniel 10:1 (biyn chain — RESOLUTION)
Hebrew: וּבִין אֶת־הַדָּבָר וּבִינָה לוֹ בַּמַּרְאֶה

Key words:
- וּבִין (u-biyn) — Conj + Verb.Qal.Perf.3ms of בין — "and he understood"
- הַדָּבָר (had-davar) — Art + Noun.ms.Abs — "the word/thing"
- וּבִינָה (u-biynah) — Conj + Noun.fs.Abs — "and understanding"
- בַּמַּרְאֶה (bam-mar'eh) — Prep + Art + Noun.ms.Abs — "in the mar'eh"

RESOLUTION: "He understood the thing, and had understanding of the MAR'EH." What was not understood in 8:27 is now understood.

## Daniel 7:1 (Aramaic — hinge chapter opening)
Aramaic: בִּשְׁנַת חֲדָה לְבֵלְאַשַׁצַּר מֶלֶךְ בָּבֶל דָּנִיֵּאל חֵלֶם חֲזָה וְחֶזְוֵי רֵאשֵׁהּ עַל־מִשְׁכְּבֵהּ

Key words:
- חֵלֶם (chelem) — Noun.ms.Abs (A2493) — "dream" (Aramaic)
- חֲזָה (chaza) — Verb.Peal.Perf.3ms (A2370) — "saw" (Aramaic cognate of chazah)
- חֶזְוֵי (chezwey) — Noun.mp.Cst (A2376) — "visions of" (Aramaic cognate of chazon)

Dan 7:1 uses BOTH Aramaic dream AND vision vocabulary, confirming its transitional genre.
