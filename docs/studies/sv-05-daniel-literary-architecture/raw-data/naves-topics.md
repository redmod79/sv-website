# Raw Nave's Topic Data

## DANIEL
- A Jewish captive, also called BELTESHAZZAR
  - Educated at king's court DAN 1
  - Interprets visions DAN 2; 4; 5
  - Promotion and executive authority of DAN 2:48,49; 5:11,29; 6:2
  - Conspiracy against, cast into the lions' den DAN 6
  - Prophecies of DAN 4:8,9; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12; MAT 24:15
  - Abstinence of DAN 1:8-16
  - Wisdom of DAN 1:17; EZK 28:3
  - Devoutness of DAN 2:18; 6; 9; 10; 12; EZK 14:14
  - Courage and fidelity of DAN 4:27; 5:17-23; 6:10-23

## VISION
A mode of revelation NUM 12:6; 1SA 3:1; 2CH 26:5; PSA 89:19; PRO 29:18; JER 14:14; 23:16; DAN 1:17; HOS 12:10; JOL 2:28; OBA 1:1; HAB 2:2; ACT 2:17
Of Daniel:
- Of the four beasts DAN 7
- Of the Ancient of Days DAN 7:9-27
- Of the ram and the he-goat DAN 8
- Of the angel DAN 10
