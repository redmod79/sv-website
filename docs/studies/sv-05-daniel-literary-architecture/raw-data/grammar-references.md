### Passage Analyses (from grammar-reference/passages/)

1. **dan-8-15-16.md** — Gabriel Commissioned
   - Compound wayhî + infinitive-construct temporal frame
   - Piel wayyiqtol 1cs with paragogic-ה (Danielic long-form)
   - The mar'eh/chazon lexical switch: chazon = whole vision-complex; mar'eh = specific visible element
   - Hiphil imperative 2ms הָבֵן — command to Gabriel about Daniel regarding the mar'eh
   - Presentative hinneh + active participle construction

2. **dan-8-26.md** — Authentication-and-Sealing Formula
   - מַרְאֵה הָעֶרֶב וְהַבֹּקֶר — singular titular unit for the 2300 evening-morning oracle
   - סְתֹם הֶחָזוֹן כִּי לְיָמִים רַבִּים — Qal imperative of סתם + causal כִּי + verbless temporal predication
   - Two verbless clauses bracketing a single imperative
   - Authentication (NmCl: mar'eh = true) → Command (Impv: seal chazon) → Ground (NmCl: for many days)

3. **dan-10-1.md** — Resolution of Understanding
   - נִגְלָה (Niphal perf 3ms of galah) — "was revealed" — passive voice signals divine agency
   - וּבִין (Qal perf 3ms of biyn) — "and he understood" — RESOLUTION of the biyn chain
   - וּבִינָה לוֹ בַּמַּרְאֶה — "and understanding to him in the mar'eh" — nominal clause
   - Six-clause verse architecture: Time → Revelation → Name → Authentication → Understanding → Mar'eh resolution

4. **dan-7-1.md** — Hinge Chapter Opening (Aramaic)
   - חֵלֶם חֲזָה — "a dream he saw" — both dream and vision vocabulary
   - חֶזְוֵי רֵאשֵׁהּ — "visions of his head" — construct chain
   - Written then spoken: כְתַב... אֲמַר — dual recording formula
   - Aramaic vocabulary: chelem (dream) + chezev (vision) = dream-vision genre
