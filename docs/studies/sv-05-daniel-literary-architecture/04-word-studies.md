### chazon — H2377 (Vision/Panoramic Revelation)
**Source:** `D:/bible/bible-studies/word-studies/H2377-chazon.md`

- **Word:** חָזוֹן | **Transliteration:** chazon | **POS:** Masculine noun
- **Root:** H2372 chazah (to gaze, perceive in vision)
- **Occurrences:** 37 in OT; Daniel = 12 (32% of all OT occurrences)
- **Semantic domain:** Prophetic/apocalyptic revelation — the divinely revealed content
- **Key distinction from mar'eh:** chazon = the revelatory CONTENT; mar'eh = the visual APPEARANCE
- **LXX primary equivalent:** ὅρασις (horasis) G3706 (28x, ~76%)
- **Daniel occurrences as "the vision" (H2377):** Dan 8:13, 8:15, 8:17, 8:26, 9:24, 10:14, 11:14
- **All Daniel chazon occurrences (12x):** Dan 1:17, 8:1, 8:2, 8:13, 8:15, 8:17, 8:26, 9:21, 9:24, 10:14, 11:14 + one in Aramaic section as chezev (Dan 7)
- **Collocations with biyn:** 3x — understanding the vision (Daniel specifically)

### mar'eh — H4758 (Appearance/Sight/Vision)
**Source:** `D:/bible/bible-studies/word-studies/H4758-mareh.md`

- **Word:** מַרְאֶה | **Transliteration:** mar'eh | **POS:** Masculine noun
- **Root:** H7200 ra'ah (to see — primitive root)
- **Occurrences:** 110 tokens across 82 verses; Daniel = 11 (3.1 per 100 verses)
- **Semantic range:** Physical attractiveness, facial countenance, clinical inspection (Lev 13), theophanic appearances, prophetic visions
- **Key distinction from chazon:** mar'eh = what is SEEN (the visual form/appearance); chazon = the revealed CONTENT
- **LXX primary equivalent:** ὄψις (opsis) G3799 for appearance; ὅρασις (horasis) G3706 for vision-sense
- **Daniel as "the vision" (H4758):** Dan 8:16, 8:27, 9:23 (+ Dan 10:1 as "the vision")
- **Critical LXX note:** LXX erases the Hebrew chazon/mar'eh distinction — both rendered ὅρασις in prophetic contexts

**The chazon/mar'eh distribution in Daniel 8:**
| Verse | Term | Context |
|-------|------|---------|
| 8:1 | chazon | "a vision appeared unto me" |
| 8:2 | chazon | "I saw in a vision" |
| 8:5 | — | Daniel "considering" (biyn) |
| 8:13 | chazon | "how long the vision concerning the tamid" |
| 8:15 | chazon + mar'eh | "had seen the chazon... as the mar'eh of a man" |
| 8:16 | mar'eh | Gabriel commanded: "make him understand the MAR'EH" |
| 8:17 | chazon | "at the time of the end shall be the chazon" |
| 8:26 | mar'eh + chazon | "the MAR'EH of the evening and morning is true... seal the CHAZON" |
| 8:27 | mar'eh | "astonished at the MAR'EH, and none understood" |

**Key finding:** The shift at v.16 is not absolute — chazon still appears at v.17. But the object of Gabriel's commission and Daniel's non-understanding is consistently mar'eh (8:16, 8:27, 9:23, 10:1).

|---------|---------------|----------------|

**Observation:** Both terms appear throughout Dan 8-10, but their REFERENTS differ:
- chazon consistently refers to the broad panoramic vision-complex
- mar'eh consistently refers to the specific element that Gabriel is sent to explain (the temporal element, the 2300 evening-mornings)
- Dan 8:26 is the proof text: mar'eh authenticated as true, chazon commanded to be sealed
