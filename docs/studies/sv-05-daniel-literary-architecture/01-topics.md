# Topics Research

## Question
What is the literary structure of Daniel's prophetic chapters? Map the Aramaic/Hebrew language division, Aramaic chiastic structure, four parallel vision cycles, chazon/mar'eh vocabulary distinction, and biyn chain. How does Daniel 7 function as a hinge?

## Topics

### DANIEL
- A Jewish captive, also called BELTESHAZZAR
  - Educated at king's court DAN 1
  - **Interprets visions DAN 2; 4; 5** ← interpreter role (Dan 2-6)
  - Promotion and executive authority of DAN 2:48,49; 5:11,29; 6:2
  - Conspiracy against, cast into the lions' den DAN 6
  - **Prophecies of DAN 4:8,9; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12; MAT 24:15** ← prophet role (Dan 7-12)
  - Abstinence of DAN 1:8-16
  - **Wisdom of DAN 1:17; EZK 28:3** ← biyn chain origin
  - Devoutness of DAN 2:18; 6; 9; 10; 12; EZK 14:14
  - Courage and fidelity of DAN 4:27; 5:17-23; 6:10-23
  - Worshiped by Nebuchadnezzar DAN 2:6

**Key structural observation:** Nave's distinguishes "Interprets visions DAN 2; 4; 5" from "Prophecies of DAN 4:8,9; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12." The shift from interpreter to prophet-recipient occurs at Daniel 7 — confirming its hinge function.

### VISION
A mode of revelation NUM 12:6; 1SA 3:1; 2CH 26:5; PSA 89:19; PRO 29:18; JER 14:14; 23:16; DAN 1:17; HOS 12:10; JOL 2:28; OBA 1:1; HAB 2:2; ACT 2:17

**Of Daniel (listed as separate vision units):**
- Of the four beasts DAN 7
- Of the Ancient of Days DAN 7:9-27
- Of the ram and the he-goat DAN 8
- Of the angel DAN 10

**Key structural observation:** Nave's categorizes Daniel's visions into four distinct units — corresponding to the four vision cycles (Dan 2 is listed under DREAM, Dan 7 under both DREAM and VISION, Dan 8 and 10-12 under VISION). The genre shift from dream to vision is reflected in the categorization.

### GABRIEL
(A messenger of God)
Appeared to:
- Daniel DAN 8:16; 9:21
- Zacharias LUK 1:11-19
- Mary LUK 1:26-29

**Key structural observation:** Gabriel appears only in Dan 8:16 and 9:21 within Daniel — confirming the biyn chain link between chapters 8 and 9.

### PROPHECY
- Inspired ISA 28:22; LUK 1:70; 2TI 3:16; 2PE 1:21
- Sure fulfillment of EZK 12:22-25,28; HAB 2:3; MAT 5:18; 24:35; ACT 13:27,29
- Cessation of LAM 2:9
- Captivity of the Jews predicted and fulfilled JER 25:11,12; 29:10,14; 32:3-5; DAN 9:2; with 2KI 25:1-8; EZR 1

**Key structural observation:** Dan 9:2 is listed under fulfilled prophecy — Daniel reading Jeremiah's 70-year prophecy, which triggers the biyn quest (stage 4 of the chain).

### DREAM
- Evanescent JOB 20:8; Vanity of ECC 5:3,7
- Of Daniel: concerning the four beasts DAN 7
- Revelations by NUM 12:6; JER 23:28; JOL 2:28; ACT 2:17
- Interpreted by Daniel DAN 2:16-23,28-30; 4

**Key structural observation:** Dan 7 is listed under DREAM ("concerning the four beasts"), while Dan 8 and 10 are not — they are under VISION. This confirms the genre progression: Dan 2 (dream interpreted by Daniel), Dan 7 (Daniel's own dream-vision), Dan 8+ (visions).

### PROPHETS
- Called SEERS 1SA 9:19; 2SA 15:27; 24:11; 2KI 17:13; 1CH 9:22; 29:29; 2CH 9:29; 12:15; 29:30; ISA 30:10; MIC 3:7
- Inspiration of: DAN 1:17; DAN 2:19; DAN 7:16; DAN 8:16; DAN 9:1-22; DAN 10:7-9

**Key structural observation:** Under "Inspiration of," Nave's lists the Daniel references in sequence that maps the biyn chain: 1:17 (capacity) → 2:19 (dream revelation) → 7:16 (angel interpretation) → 8:16 (Gabriel commissioned) → 9:1-22 (understanding sought and given) → 10:7-9 (final vision).

### REVELATION
- EXO 3:1-6,14; 1CH 28:11-19; MAT 3:17; MAT 16:17
- Mode of: visions (NUM 12:6), dreams, prophets

### HEBREW
- GEN 14:13; JHN 5:2; ACT 21:40; REV 9:11
- Language references confirm Hebrew as a distinct language category, relevant for the Aramaic/Hebrew division in Daniel.
