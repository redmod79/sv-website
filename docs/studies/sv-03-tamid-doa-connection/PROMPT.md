# Bible Study: The Daily-to-Annual Sanctuary System — Tamid and Day of Atonement

## Question
What is the connection between the daily tamid service and the Day of Atonement in Leviticus? How does the daily service transfer sin INTO the sanctuary (Lev 4:5-7, 10:17, 15:31)? How does the DOA take sin OUT (Lev 16:14-22)? What is the vocabulary distinction — kaphar+salach (daily, forgiveness) vs kaphar+taher (annual, cleansing)? How does the nasa verb connect the daily priest (Lev 10:17) and the annual scapegoat (Lev 16:22)? Why does the tamid never stop, even on the DOA (Num 29:11)?
