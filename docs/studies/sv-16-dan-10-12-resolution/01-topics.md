- Conspiracy against, cast into the lions' den (DAN 6)
- Prophecies of (DAN 4:8,9; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12; MAT 24:15)

- Wisdom of (DAN 1:17; EZK 28:3)
- Devoutness of (DAN 2:18; 6; 9; 10; 12; EZK 14:14)
- Courage and fidelity (DAN 4:27; 5:17-23; 6:10-23)

### MICHAEL

### FASTING
- Accompanied by prayer (DAN 9:3)
- Prolonged for three weeks by Daniel (DAN 10:2,3)
- For forty days by Moses (EXO 24:18; 34:28; DEU 9:9,18), Elijah (1KI 19:8), Jesus (MAT 4:2)
- Daniel's instances:
