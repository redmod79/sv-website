### Critical Parsing Notes for Dan 12:7

1. **וַיָּרֶם** (wayyarem): Hiphil Wayyiqtol 3ms of רום (rum, "to be high") — "and he lifted/raised." The Hiphil causative: he caused [his hands] to go up. Wayyiqtol narrative sequence: this action follows the hearing.

2. **יְמִינוֹ וּשְׂמֹאלוֹ** (yemino u-smolo): "his right [hand] and his left [hand]." BOTH hands raised — contrast with Rev 10:5 where only the RIGHT hand is raised (ten dexian). The raising of both hands intensifies the oath formula (cf. Deut 32:40 where God lifts His hand in singular).

3. **וַיִּשָּׁבַע** (wayyishaba): Niphal Wayyiqtol 3ms of שׁבע (shaba, H7650) — "and he swore." The Niphal reflexive: "he sevened himself" / "he bound himself by oath." The wayyiqtol continues the narrative sequence after the hand-raising.

4. **בְּחֵי הָעוֹלָם** (be-chey ha-olam): "by the living [one] of eternity" — the oath mediator formula. Construct chain: chey (living, adjective ms construct of חי H2416) + ha-olam (the eternity, noun ms absolute of עולם H5769). This is the identical formula behind the Greek ἐν τῷ ζῶντι εἰς τοὺς αἰῶνας τῶν αἰώνων in Rev 10:6.

5. **לְמוֹעֵד מוֹעֲדִים וָחֵצִי** (le-moed moedim va-chetsi): "for an appointed time, appointed times, and a half." The oath CONTENT — a time duration. Moed (H4150) = appointed time/meeting. The formula: singular + plural + half = 1 + 2 + ½ = 3½ time units.

6. **וּכְכַלּוֹת נַפֵּץ יַד־עַם־קֹדֶשׁ** (u-khe-khallot nappets yad-am-qodesh): "and when the shattering of the hand/power of the holy people is complete." Two Piel infinitive constructs chained: kallot (to finish, Piel Inf. Cst. of כלה H3615) + nappets (to shatter, Piel Inf. Cst. of נפץ H5310). The construct chain yad-am-qodesh = "hand of people of holiness" = the power of the holy people.

7. **תִּכְלֶינָה כָל־אֵלֶּה** (tikhleynah kol-elleh): "all these things shall be finished." Qal Imperfect 3fp of כלה (kalah) — the feminine plural agrees with the implied feminine subject (the wonders/things asked about in v.6). This is the TERMINUS: when the shattering is complete, all these things finish.

### Structural Comparison: Dan 12:7 vs. Rev 10:5-6

| Element | Daniel 12:7 (Hebrew) | Revelation 10:5-6 (Greek) |
|---------|---------------------|--------------------------|
| Figure | "the man clothed in linen" (ha-ish levush ha-baddim) | "the angel" (ho angelos) |
| Position | "above the waters of the river" (mimma'al le-meymey ha-ye'or) | "standing upon the sea and upon the earth" (hestota epi tes thalasses kai epi tes ges) |
| Hands | BOTH hands raised (yemino u-smolo) | RIGHT hand only (ten dexian) |
| Direction | "unto heaven" (el ha-shamayim) | "to heaven" (eis ton ouranon) |
| Oath verb | wayyishaba (Niphal Wayq. 3ms of shaba H7650) | omosen (Aorist Act. Ind. 3s of omnyo G3660) |
| Oath mediator | "by the living one of eternity" (be-chey ha-olam) | "by the one living unto the ages of the ages" (en to zonti eis tous aionas ton aionon) |
| Content | DURATION: "a time, times, and a half" (moed moedim va-chetsi) | TERMINATION: "time shall be no longer" (chronos ouketi estai) |
| Terminus | "all these things shall be finished" (tikhleynah kol-elleh) | "the mystery of God should be finished" (etelesthe to mysterion tou theou) |
