# Hebrew Parsing Key Findings

## Lev 25:9 — Jubilee Trumpet
- הַֽעֲבַרְתָּ (Hiphil.Perf.2ms of עבר H5674) — "you shall cause to pass" the trumpet
- שׁוֹפַר תְּרוּעָה — "horn of shouting" (construct chain)
- בְּיוֹם הַכִּפֻּרִים — "on the day of atonement" (explicit DOA date)
- תַּעֲבִירוּ (Hiphil.Impf.2mp of עבר) — "you shall cause to pass" (second Hiphil avar)
- BOTH verbs = Hiphil of avar — same form as Zech 3:4

## Lev 25:10 — Jubilee Proclamation
- וּקְרָאתֶם דְּרוֹר — "and you shall proclaim liberty" (Qal.Perf.2mp of קרא + דרור H1865)
- יוֹבֵל — parsed as noun.ms.Abs from H3104
- וְשַׁבְתֶּם... תָּשֻׁבוּ — double שׁוב (return)

## Isa 61:1 — Messianic Anointing
- משׁח (Qal.Perf.3ms) — "anointed" (messiah verb)
- לִקְרֹא לִשְׁבוּיִם דְּרוֹר — "to proclaim to captives deror" (H1865, same as Lev 25:10)

## Isa 61:2 — DOA Binary
- שְׁנַת רָצוֹן (H7522) — "year of favor"
- יוֹם נָקָם (H5359) — "day of vengeance"
- ratson (favor/acceptance) paired with naqam (vengeance)

## Isa 61:3 — Trees of Righteousness
- אֵילֵי הַצֶּדֶק — "oaks/mighty trees of THE tsedeq" (H6664)

## Isa 61:8 — God Loves Justice
- אֹהֵב מִשְׁפָּט (H4941) — "loving judgment" (Qal ptcp)
- בְרִית עוֹלָם אֶכְרוֹת — "everlasting covenant I will cut" (karath!)

## Isa 61:10 — Priestly Investiture
- הִלְבִּישַׁנִי בִּגְדֵי יֶשַׁע — Hiphil of labash + H3444 yeshuah
- מְעִיל צְדָקָה — "robe of tsedaqah" (H6666)
- כֶּחָתָן יְכַהֵן פְּאֵר — Piel of kahan = "acts as priest"

## Isa 61:11 — Sprouting Righteousness
- יַצְמִיחַ צְדָקָה — Hiphil of tsamach + H6666
- נֶגֶד כָּל הַגּוֹיִם — "before all the nations"

## Jer 34:15 — Deror = Yashar
- וַתַּעֲשׂוּ אֶת הַיָּשָׁר (H3477) — "you did the right/yashar"
- בְּעֵינַי — "in my eyes"
- לִקְרֹא דְרוֹר (H1865) — "to proclaim deror"

## Jer 34:17 — Anti-Jubilee
- Two uses of דרור: what they refused + God's ironic counter-proclamation
- "I proclaim a deror FOR YOU to the sword, pestilence, famine"

## Zec 3:4 — Iniquity Removal + Investiture
- הֶעֱבַרְתִּי (Hiphil.Perf.1s of עבר H5674) — "I have caused to pass" = "I have removed"
- עֲוֹנֶךָ — "your iniquity"
- וְהַלְבֵּשׁ (Hiphil InfAbs of labash) — divine causative clothing
- מַחֲלָצוֹת (H4254) — "festival garments"

## Zec 3:5 — DOA Vocabulary in Garment Scene
- צָנִיף טָהוֹר — "turban PURE/CLEAN" (tahor H2891 = DOA vocabulary!)
- יַּלְבִּשֻׁהוּ (Hiphil of labash) — "they clothed him"

## Lev 23:29 — DOA Penalty
- לֹא תְעֻנֶּה — Pual.Impf.3fs of anah = "not be afflicted" (passive)
- וְנִכְרְתָה — Niphal.Perf.3fs of karath = "cut off" (divine passive)

## Lev 23:40 — Tabernacles Joy
- וּשְׂמַחְתֶּם (Qal.Perf.2mp of samach) — "you shall rejoice"
- לִפְנֵי יְהוָה — "before YHWH"
