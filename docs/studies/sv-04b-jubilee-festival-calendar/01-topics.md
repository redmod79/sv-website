# Topics — sv-04b Jubilee, Isaiah 61, and the Festival Calendar

## Primary Topics

### JUBILEE (Nave's)
- Called "the acceptable year of the LORD" (Isa 61:2)
- Called "the year of liberty" (Ezek 46:17)
- Laws concerning: Lev 25:8-55; 27:17-24; Num 36:4
- Proclaimed ON the Day of Atonement: Lev 25:9
- Three restoration acts: debts cancelled, slaves freed, land returned (Lev 25:10,13,39-41)
- Key verses: Lev 25:8-17, Lev 25:39-55, Lev 27:17-24, Num 36:4, Eze 46:17

### LIBERTY (Nave's)
- Proclaimed in year of Jubilee: Lev 25:10; Jer 34:8,15-17
- Figurative: Isa 61:2; 63:4; Luk 4:19; Jhn 8:32,33,36; Gal 3:28; Eph 6:8; Col 3:11
- Jesus quotes Isa 61:1-2 in Luke 4:18-19 — declares fulfillment

### TABERNACLES, FEAST OF (Nave's)
- Instituted: Exo 23:16; 34:22; Lev 23:34-43
- Design of: Lev 23:42-43 (dwell in booths, remember exodus)
- Commanded rejoicing: Lev 23:40 (samach before YHWH 7 days)
- Tishri 15-22 — five days after DOA (Tishri 10)
- Law read every 7th year: Deu 31:10-12; Neh 8:18
- Post-captivity observance: Ezr 3:4; Neh 8:14-18
- Eschatological: Zec 14:16-19 (all nations observe or face drought)
- Penalty for non-observance: Zec 14:17-18

### TRUMPETS (Nave's)
- Feast of Trumpets: Lev 23:24-25; Num 29:1-6
- Tishri 1 — the alarm that opens the Tishri sequence
- zikron teruah — "memorial of blowing" (Lev 23:24)
- Post-captivity celebration: Neh 8:2,9-12
- Functions of trumpets: Num 10:1-10 (assembly, alarm, journeying, sacrifice, joy)

### ATONEMENT, DAY OF (Nave's)
- Time: Exo 30:10; Lev 23:27; 25:9; Num 29:7
- How observed: Exo 30:10; Lev 16:2-34; 23:27-32; Num 29:7-11
- Tishri 10 — between Trumpets (Tishri 1) and Tabernacles (Tishri 15)
- Jubilee proclaimed ON the DOA: Lev 25:9
- Required affliction: Lev 16:29,31; 23:27,32
- Penalty for non-participation: Lev 23:29-30 (karath/abad)
- Typified: Lev 16:30,34; 17:11

## Supporting Topics

### RIGHTEOUSNESS (Nave's)
- Imputed: Gen 15:6; Rom 4:3-5
- Garments of: Isa 61:10 (meil tsedaqah); Rev 19:8
- Everlasting: Dan 9:24 (tsedeq olamim); Psa 119:142
- Trees of: Isa 61:3 (eiley hatsedeq)
- Before all nations: Isa 61:11

### VENGEANCE (Nave's)
- Belongs to God: Deu 32:35; Rom 12:19
- Day of: Isa 34:8; 61:2; 63:4
- Paired with favor: Isa 61:2 (ratson + naqam)
- Eschatological: Rev 6:10; 19:2

### RESTORATION (Semantic search)
- Jubilee = restoration to original state (Lev 25:10,13)
- Acts 3:21 "restitution of all things" — NT Jubilee echo
- Shub (return) used twice in Lev 25:10 — return to property, return to family
