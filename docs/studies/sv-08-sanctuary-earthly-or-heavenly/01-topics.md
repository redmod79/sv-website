# Topics Research

## Question
Is the sanctuary in Daniel 8:11-14 the earthly or heavenly sanctuary? Examine: the 3ms suffix on miqdasho, the mekon (H4349) vocabulary, the sar-ha-tsaba identity, and whether the horn's actions can reach the heavenly realm. How does Hebrews 8:1-2 inform this question?

## Topics

### TABERNACLE
- One existed before Moses received the pattern authorized on Mount Sinai (Exo 33:7-11)
- The one instituted by Moses was called:
  - SANCTUARY (Exo 25:8)
  - TABERNACLE / TENT OF MEETING (Exo 27:21)
  - OF TESTIMONY (Exo 38:21; Num 1:50)
  - TEMPLE OF THE LORD (1 Sam 1:9; 3:3)
  - HOUSE OF THE LORD (Jos 6:24)
- Pattern of, revealed to Moses (Exo 25:9; 26:30; 39:32,42,43; Act 7:44; Heb 8:5)
- Materials voluntarily offered (Exo 25:1-8; 35:4-29; 36:3-7)
- Description: frame (Exo 26:15-37), outer covering (Exo 25:5; 26:7-14), curtains (Exo 26:1-14,31-37)
- The Holy Place (Exo 26:31-37; 40:22-26; Heb 9:2-6,8)
- The Most Holy Place (Exo 26:33-35; 40:20,21; Heb 9:3-5,7,8)
- Filled with the cloud of glory (Exo 40:34-38)
- Defilement of, punished (Lev 15:31; Num 19:13,20; Eze 5:11; 23:38)
- Symbol of spiritual things (Psa 15:1; Heb 8:2,5; 9:1-12,24)

### SANCTUARY
- The Holy of Holies (Heb 9:2)
- Divine dwelling place (Exo 25:8)
- Reverence for (Lev 19:30; 26:2)
- In the charge of high priest (Exo 27:21; Lev 24:3; Num 18:5)
- The Holy Place in the temple (Lam 2:7,20; Eze 42:20)
- FIGURATIVE (Eze 11:16)
- Symbolical (Heb 8:2,5)

### TEMPLE (Solomon's — selected entries)
