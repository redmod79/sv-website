**Key observation:** Dan 7:8 uses common Aramaic adjective ze'irah for "little." Compare Dan 8:9 which uses Hebrew hapax noun mits'eirah.

## Daniel 8:8-9 (Hebrew — Horn Emergence in Dan 8)

### Dan 8:8:
- צְפִיר (tsephir) Noun.ms.Cst = "he-goat"
- הִגְדִּיל (higdil) Verb.Hiphil.Perf.3ms = "he magnified" — gadal H1431
- עַד מְאֹד (ad meod) = "exceedingly"
- כְּעָצְמוֹ (ke-otsmo) = "when he was strong" — atsam Qal InfCon
- נִשְׁבְּרָה (nishberah) Verb.Niphal.Perf.3fs = "was broken" — passive
- הַקֶּרֶן הַגְּדוֹלָה (ha-qeren ha-gedolah) = "the great horn"
- וַתַּעֲלֶנָה (vatta'alenah) Verb.Qal.Wayq.3fp = "and they came up"
- חָזוּת אַרְבַּע (chazut arba) = "four conspicuous/notable ones"

### Dan 8:9:
- מִן הָאַחַת מֵהֶם (min ha-achat mehem) = "from one of them" — 3mp suffix on fem antecedents = constructio ad sensum
- יָצָא (yatsa) Verb.Qal.Perf.3ms = "he went out" — MASCULINE verb for a feminine noun (qeren)
- קֶרֶן אַחַת (qeren achat) = "one horn"
- מִצְּעִירָה (mits-tse'irah) Noun.fs.Abs = "from littleness/smallness" — H4704 HAPAX
- וַתִּגְדַּל (vattigdal) Verb.Qal.Wayq.3fs = "and she grew great" — returns to feminine
- יֶתֶר (yether) Noun.ms.Abs = "exceedingly/beyond" — H3499 adverbial
- אֶל הַנֶּגֶב (el ha-negev) = "toward the south"
- אֶל הַמִּזְרָח (el ha-mizrach) = "toward the east"
- אֶל הַצֶּבִי (el ha-tsebi) = "toward the pleasant/beautiful [land]"

**Key observation:** The masculine verb yatsa (3ms) with a feminine subject qeren signals constructio ad sensum — the horn is grammatically treated as the male KING it represents. The 3mp suffix mehem ("of them") on feminine antecedents (horns or winds, both feminine) uses the same construction.

## Daniel 8:10-13 (Hebrew — Four-Fold Attack)

### Dan 8:10:
- וַתִּגְדַּל (vattigdal) Verb.Qal.Wayq.3fs = "and it grew great" — gadal Qal
- צְבָא הַשָּׁמַיִם (tseva ha-shamayim) = "host of the heavens" — construct chain
- וַתַּפֵּל (vattappel) Verb.Hiphil.Wayq.3fs = "and it caused to fall" — Hiphil causative
- מִן הַצָּבָא (min ha-tsava) = "some of the host"
- מִן הַכּוֹכָבִים (min ha-kokhavim) = "some of the stars"
- וַתִּרְמְסֵם (vattirm'sem) Verb.Qal.Wayq.3fs+3mp = "and it trampled them"

### Dan 8:11:
- שַׂר הַצָּבָא (sar ha-tsava) = "Prince of the host" — construct chain
- הִגְדִּיל (higdil) Verb.Hiphil.Perf.3ms = "he magnified [himself]" — shifts to MASCULINE, Hiphil
- הוּרַם (huram) Verb.Hophal.Perf.3ms = "was taken away/lifted" — PASSIVE, from ruwm H7311
- הַתָּמִיד (ha-tamid) = "the continual [service]" — tamid H8548
- וְהֻשְׁלַךְ (ve-hushlakh) Verb.Hophal.Perf.3ms = "and was cast down" — PASSIVE, from shalak H7993
- מְכוֹן מִקְדָּשׁוֹ (mekhon miqdash-o) = "the place of HIS sanctuary" — 3ms suffix

### Dan 8:12:
- וְצָבָא תִּנָּתֵן (ve-tsava tinnatein) Verb.Niphal.Impf.3fs = "and a host was given" — divine PASSIVE
- עַל הַתָּמִיד (al ha-tamid) = "against the daily [service]"
- בְּפָשַׁע (be-fesha) = "by reason of transgression" — pesha H6586
- וְתַשְׁלֵךְ אֱמֶת אַרְצָה (ve-tashlekh emet artsah) = "and it cast truth to the ground" — Hiphil ACTIVE
- וְעָשְׂתָה (ve-asetah) = "and it practised"
- וְהִצְלִיחָה (ve-hitslychah) = "and it prospered" — tsalach H6743 Hiphil

### Dan 8:13:
- חָזוֹן (chazon) = "vision" — H2377
- הַתָּמִיד (ha-tamid) = "the daily"
- וְהַפֶּשַׁע שֹׁמֵם (ve-ha-pesha shomem) = "and the transgression of desolation" — pesha + shamem Qal Ptcp
- תֵּת (tet) = "to give" (Qal InfCon of natan)
- וְקֹדֶשׁ (ve-qodesh) = "and sanctuary/holiness"
- וְצָבָא (ve-tsava) = "and host"
- מִרְמָס (mirmas) = "trampling"

**Key: Voice Chiasm in Dan 8:11-12 (Active-Passive-Active):**
- v.11a: ACTIVE (Hiphil higdil — he magnified himself)
- v.11b: PASSIVE (Hophal huram — was taken away; Hophal hushlakh — was cast down)
- v.12a: PASSIVE (Niphal tinnatein — was given)
- v.12b: ACTIVE (Hiphil tashlekh — it cast down truth)
