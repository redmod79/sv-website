# Bible Study: Are the Little Horns of Daniel 7 and Daniel 8 the Same Entity?

## Question
Are the little horns of Daniel 7 and Daniel 8 the same entity? What textual evidence connects them? Compare shared characteristics, shared targets, shared timeline, and shared vocabulary. How does progressive revelation intensify from chapter 7 to chapter 8? Why are both visions needed?

## Prior Research Summary
