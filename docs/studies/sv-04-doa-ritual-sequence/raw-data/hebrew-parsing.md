## Lev 16:16 — Three-Term Formula

## Lev 16:17 — Sanctuary Exclusion (WXYq construction)
```

Key parsing:
- vekhol-adam (Conj + kol Noun.ms.Cst + adam Noun.ms.Abs) = "and all mankind"
- lo yihyeh (Neg + hayah Qal.Impf.3ms) = "shall not be"
- be'ohel moed = "in the tent of meeting"

This clause type is grammatically marked as distinctive.

Triple beneficiary: ba'ado (for himself) + ube'ad beito (for his household) + ube'ad kol-qehal yisrael (for all the congregation of Israel)
```

```
Key:
- lo te'unneh (Pual.Impf.3fs anah) = "shall not be afflicted" — PASSIVE
- venikhretah (Niphal.Perf.3fs karath) = "and she shall be cut off" — PASSIVE (divine)
- me'ammeha = "from her peoples"
```
