# Topics Research

## Question
Who are the maskilim of Daniel 11:33, 11:35, 12:3, and 12:10? What does sakal (H7919) mean vs biyn (H995)? How does Dan 12:10's promise complete the biyn chain from 8:16? What characterizes this community and how do they participate in vindication?

### RIGHTEOUS

**Compared with:**
- The sun: JDG 5:31; MAT 13:43
- Stars: DAN 12:3
- Lights: MAT 5:14; PHP 2:15

- Gold: JOB 23:10; LAM 4:2

**Described:** EXO 33:16; PSA 1:1,2; 15:1-5; 24:3-5; 112:1-10; PRO 2:9-12; 4:18; ISA 33:15,16; 51:1; 60:21; MAT 5:3-10,13-16; PHP 2:15; REV 14:4,5

**Key for this study:** The righteous are explicitly compared to stars (DAN 12:3), the sun (JDG 5:31; MAT 13:43), and lights (MAT 5:14; PHP 2:15). These are the same images used for the maskilim.

### WISDOM (Spiritual)

**From God:** DEU 4:5,6,35,36; DAN 1:17; 2:20-23; MAT 11:25-27; 13:11

**Key for this study:** Daniel 11:32-33 and 12:3,4,10 are explicitly indexed under WISDOM. Hosea 14:9 ("Who is wise, and he shall understand these things?") provides the morally-conditioned understanding pattern. Proverbs 28:5 ("they that seek the LORD understand all things") parallels Dan 12:10's binary.

### PERSECUTION (of the Righteous)
**Source:** naves_db.py --topic "PERSECUTION"

**Of the righteous:** PSA 119:51,61,69,78,85-87,95,110,157,161; MAT 5:10-12,44; 10:16-18,21-23; 23:34,35; 24:8-10; LUK 21:12-19; HEB 10:32-34; 11:25-27,33-38; REV 2:3,10,13; 6:9-11; 12:11; 17:6; 20:4

**Instances:** Shadrach, Meshach, Abed-nego (DAN 3:8-23); Daniel (DAN 6); prophets (MAT 21:35,36)

**Key for this study:** Dan 11:33 describes the maskilim falling "by the sword, and by flame, by captivity, and by spoil, many days" — persecution language that matches this topic. The persecution of the maskilim is not incidental but formative.

### PURIFICATION
**Source:** naves_db.py --topic "PURIFICATION"

**By fire:** NUM 31:23
**By blood:** EXO 24:5-8; LEV 14:6,7; HEB 9:12-14,19-22
**Figurative:** PSA 26:6; 51:7; EZK 36:25; HEB 10:22

**Key for this study:** The purification triad (barar, laban, tsaraph) in Dan 11:35 and 12:10 uses refining/whitening imagery. The figurative category connects to the maskilim's formation process.

### TEACHERS
**Source:** naves_db.py --topic "TEACHERS"

- Samuel, head of school of prophets: 1SA 19:20
- Elisha, head of, at Gilgal: 2KI 4:38
- False teachers: DEU 13:1-3; MAT 5:19; 7:15

**Key for this study:** The maskilim's instructing function (Dan 11:33 "shall instruct many") makes them teachers. The contrast between true and false teachers echoes the wicked-wise binary of Dan 12:10.
