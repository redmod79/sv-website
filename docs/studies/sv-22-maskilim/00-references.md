# Reference Gathering: The Maskilim — The Wise Who Understand

## Question
Who are the maskilim of Daniel 11:33, 11:35, 12:3, and 12:10? What does sakal (H7919, insight leading to action) mean vs biyn (H995, discernment/comprehension)? The maskilim "instruct many" (11:33), endure persecution and "refinement" (11:35), "shine as the brightness of the firmament" (12:3), and "turn many to righteousness" (matsdiqey, 12:3 — same tsadaq root as nitsdaq in 8:14). How does Dan 12:10's promise ("the wise shall understand, yavinu") complete the biyn chain from 8:16? What characterizes this community — and how do they participate in vindication?

## Prior Studies

### From Series Dependencies (Integrate List)

**sv-11-sealing-system:**
- Question: "What do satham and chatham mean? What four mechanisms unseal the vision?"
- Four unsealing mechanisms: Christological (Lamb's death provides authority), temporal (seal expires at "time of the end"), moral/spiritual (character determines who understands), progressive (understanding increases gradually)
- The moral/spiritual mechanism directly defines the maskilim: "Dan 12:10 is the clearest statement: 'None of the wicked shall understand; but the wise (maskilim) shall understand.' Even after the temporal seal expires, understanding is discriminating. Character, not intellect, determines access."
- The maskilim have been "purified, and made white, and tried" (Dan 12:10) — three purification verbs (barar, laban, tsaraph) describe the preparation required
- The progressive mechanism notes: "The maskilim who 'shine as the brightness of the firmament' (Dan 12:3) are on this brightening path" — linked to Prov 4:18
- Key vocabulary: satham (H5640) blocks access; chatham (H2856) secures container; the maskilim are those who receive the opened content

**sv-16-dan-10-12-resolution:**
- Question: "Daniel 10-12 relationship to earlier visions. biyn chain resolution."
- **The biyn chain: 19 occurrences of biyn (H995) across Dan 8-12.** Tracks understanding from Daniel's desire (8:15) through failure (8:27) through resolution (10:1) through second failure on new content (12:8) to the discriminating conclusion (12:10)
- The chain's destination is NOT Daniel — it is the maskilim of the time of the end
- Dan 12:10 uses BOTH sakal and biyn in one verse: maskilim (sakal) yavinu (biyn). Hebrew distinguishes analytical discernment (biyn) from practical wisdom/insight (sakal). The LXX flattens both to syniemi
- **The purification triad** (barar, laban, tsaraph) appears as a set ONLY in Dan 11:35 and 12:10 — exclusive pairing. The historical refining (11:35) produces the eschatological identity (12:10)
- Stem shift: In 11:35 verbs are Hiphil/Piel (external forces refine the maskilim). In 12:10 verbs are Hithpael/Niphal (reflexive/passive — the many purify themselves). The shift suggests maturation
- **The matsdiqey-nitsdaq connection:** Dan 12:3's matsdiqey (Hiphil.Ptcp.Cst of tsadaq) shares the root with nitsdaq (Dan 8:14). Niphal = sanctuary receives vindication. Hiphil = maskilim cause many to be righteous. Complementary functions
- Dan 11:33: "they that understand among the people shall instruct many" — the maskilim both understand AND teach
- Dan 12:10's moral binary echoes the DOA sorting (Lev 16): those who afflict their souls participate; those who refuse are cut off
- The 1290 and 1335 are sealed signposts designed for the maskilim, not for Daniel (Dan 12:8-9)

**sv-18-vindication-meaning:**
- Question: "What does nitsdaq qodesh mean?"
- nitsdaq (Niphal of tsadaq, H6663) = the sanctuary receives vindication. The only Niphal of tsadaq in the entire OT
- matsdiqey (Hiphil of tsadaq, Dan 12:3) = the maskilim cause many to be righteous. Same root, different stems, complementary roles
- "The maskilim understand what happened (Dan 12:10) and help others see it. They expose the counterfeit. They point people to the genuine heavenly ministry."
- The vindication is not purely a heavenly transaction — it has an earthly dimension through the maskilim
- "Heaven renders the verdict. The wise implement it on earth."
- Five beneficiaries of vindication: God's people, God's character, God's ministry, God's truth, the watching universe — the maskilim participate in the "pastoral dimension for the deceived"
- The zero-sum problem (Job 40:8) is solved by substitution. Both God and His people are vindicated because a righteous substitute absorbed the penalty

**sv-20-rev-10-unsealing:**
- Question: "What is the 'little book open' in Revelation 10?"
- The little book = Daniel's sealed prophecy, now permanently unsealed (five converging evidence lines)
- The recommission (Rev 10:11): "thou must prophesy again before many peoples, nations, tongues, and kings" — this is the vehicle by which the maskilim's understanding reaches the world
- The connection to the maskilim: "Dan 12:3: the maskilim 'turn many to righteousness' (matsdiqey). Rev 10:11's universal recommission provides the vehicle: prophesying 'before many peoples, nations, tongues, and kings' is how the maskilim's understanding reaches the world."
- Sweet-then-bitter experience (Rev 10:9-10) = prophetic content received with joy whose full implications prove painful. The disappointment leads to a broader commission
- The chronos termination (Rev 10:6) marks the end of prophetic time periods — after this, the maskilim's work operates in a post-prophetic-time era

**sv-21-three-angels:**
- Question: "What is the significance of the three angels' messages in Rev 14:6-12?"
- The three angels' messages ARE the content of the "prophesy again" commission from Rev 10:11
- The nitsdaq-matsdiqey-terountes chain: Dan 8:14 (sanctuary vindicated) → Dan 12:3 (maskilim cause many to be righteous) → Rev 14:12 (saints keep commandments and faith of Jesus)
- Rev 14:12 identifies the remnant — they endure (hupomone), keep God's commandments (terountes), hold the faith of Jesus (pistin Iesou)
- "The Niphal (passive) describes what God does: He vindicates the sanctuary. The Hiphil (causative) describes what the wise do: they turn many to righteousness. terountes describes the character that results."
- The first angel's creation language (Rev 14:7 quoting Exo 20:11) directly counters the horn's attack on "times and laws" (Dan 7:25) — the maskilim's message restores what the horn dismantled
- The vindication declarations (Rev 15:3, 16:7, 19:1-2) use dikaios + alethinos pair — restoring the emeth the horn cast to the ground
