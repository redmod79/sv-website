# Raw Greek Parsing Data

## Romans 3:24
δικαιούμενοι (G1344) — V-PPP-NPM: Present Passive Participle Nom Pl M — "being justified"
δωρεάν (G1432) — ADV: "freely/as a gift"
χάριτι (G5485) — N-DSF: Dative Sg F — "by grace" (dative of means)
ἀπολυτρώσεως (G629) — N-GSF: Gen Sg F — "of the redemption"
Χριστῷ Ἰησοῦ (G5547, G2424) — N-DSM: "in Christ Jesus"
Key: Present passive = ongoing divine action; dative of means = grace as instrument.

## Romans 4:3
ἐπίστευσεν (G4100) — V-AAI-3S: Aorist Active Indicative — "he believed" (completed act)
ἐλογίσθη (G3049) — V-API-3S: Aorist Passive Indicative — "it was reckoned" (divine passive)
δικαιοσύνην (G1343) — N-ASF: Accusative Sg F — "righteousness" (goal of reckoning)
Key: Aorist passive of logizomai = divine passive (God reckoned).

## Galatians 2:16
δικαιοῦται (G1344) — V-PPI-3S: Present Passive Indicative — "is justified" (present general truth)
πίστεως Χριστοῦ (G4102, G5547) — N-GSF + N-GSM: "faith of/in Christ"
δικαιωθῶμεν (G1344) — V-APS-1P: Aorist Passive Subjunctive — "we might be justified"
δικαιωθήσεται (G1344) — V-FPI-3S: Future Passive Indicative — "shall be justified"
Key: Three forms of dikaioo in one verse — present, aorist, future — all passive (divine agent).
