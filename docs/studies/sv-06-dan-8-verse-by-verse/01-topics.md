# Topics Research

## Question
Produce a deep verse-by-verse analysis of Daniel 8:8-19. Cover: the goat-to-little-horn transition (8:8-9), the four-fold attack (8:10-12), the heavenly dialogue "how long?" (8:13), the answer "2300 evening-mornings, then the sanctuary shall be vindicated" (8:14), Daniel's quest for understanding (8:15-16), and Gabriel's framing "at the time of the end" (8:17-19).

## Topics

### GABRIEL
- A messenger of God
- Appeared to Daniel: DAN 8:16; 9:21
- Appeared to Zacharias: LUK 1:11-19
- Appeared to Mary: LUK 1:26-29

Note: Gabriel appears only 4x in Scripture (2 OT, 2 NT). Both OT appearances are in Daniel. The name means "mighty man of God" (gever + el), echoing Dan 8:15's ke-mareh gaver.

### HORN
- Literal: container for anointing oil (1SA 16:1; 1KI 1:39)
- FIGURATIVE: Divine protection (2SA 22:3); Power (1KI 22:11; PSA 89:24; 92:10; 132:17)
- SYMBOLICAL: DAN 7:7-24; 8:3-9,20; AMO 6:13; MIC 4:13; HAB 3:4; ZEC 1:18-21; REV 5:6; 12:3; 13:1,11; 17:3-16

Note: In Dan 8, horn appears as: great horn (v.5,8,21), two horns of ram (v.3,6,7,20), four conspicuous horns (v.8,22), and the little horn (v.9-12,23-25). qeren (H7161) is grammatically feminine throughout Dan 8.

### SANCTUARY
- The Holy of Holies
- Divine dwelling place: EXO 25:8
- In charge of high priest: EXO 27:21; LEV 24:3; NUM 18:5
- Holy Place in temple: LAM 2:7,20; EZK 42:20
- FIGURATIVE: EZK 11:16
- Symbolical: HEB 8:2,5

Note: Dan 8 uses two sanctuary words — miqdash (H4720, v.11, the spatial locus) and qodesh (H6944, vv.13-14, the holiness-sphere). The shift from miqdash to qodesh enlarges scope from the attacked place to the vindicated holiness-domain.

### STARS
- Created by God: GEN 1:16; JOB 26:13; PSA 8:3
- Falling of: DAN 8:10; MAT 24:29; MRK 13:25; REV 6:13; 8:10; 9:1; 12:4
- FIGURATIVE: Of the deliverer NUM 24:17; Seven stars = seven congregations REV 1:16,20; Crown of twelve stars REV 12:1

Note: In Dan 8:10, the stars (kokabim, H3556) are paralleled with the host (tsaba, H6635) of heaven. The horn casts them down (Hiphil of naphal) and tramples them. Rev 12:4 echoes: the dragon's tail drew the third part of the stars.

### JUDGMENT (from semantic search)
- Relevant references: DAN 7:9-10 (thrones cast down, Ancient of Days, books opened); ROM 14:10; 2CO 5:10; REV 20:11-15
- The Dan 7:9-10 judgment scene is the parallel vision-cycle to Dan 8:14's nitsdaq verdict.

### ANGEL (a spirit)
- Angels in Daniel's visions: DAN 4:13-17; 8:13,14; 8:16; 8:19; 9:21-27; 10:10-20
- As mediators of revelation: ZEC 1:9-11; HEB 2:2; REV 1:1

Note: The "holy ones" (qadoshim) of Dan 8:13 are angelic beings who ask and answer the "how long?" question. The palmoni (certain/unnamed one) is the responder whose answer is directed to Daniel (v.14, "to me").

## Related Topics Discovered

### PROPHECY
- Daniel's prophecies: DAN 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12
- Jesus references Daniel: MAT 24:15 ("whoso readeth, let him understand")

### HEAVEN
- God's dwelling: DEU 26:15; 1KI 8:30; PSA 11:4; 103:19; ISA 57:15
- Heavenly sanctuary: HEB 8:1; REV 12:7-9

### WRATH / INDIGNATION
- zaam (H2195) in Dan 8:19 — "the latter end of the indignation"
- The same word appears in Dan 11:36 — "prosper till the indignation be accomplished"
- This vocabulary bracket binds Dan 8:19 to Dan 11:36
