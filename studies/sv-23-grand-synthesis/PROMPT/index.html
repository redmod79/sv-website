    Bible Study: Grand Synthesis — From the Tamid to the Verdict¶

    Question¶

    Trace the complete arc from the tamid through the verdict. How do all 22 prior studies form one system? What is the one sentence that captures the entire narrative from Leviticus to Revelation?

    Prior Research Summary¶

    From 00-references.md (22 prior studies fully integrated)¶

    The reference agent read all 22 studies in the sv-series and compiled: - Complete study arc from sv-01 (Biblical Righteousness) through sv-22 (The Maskilim), each contributing a specific piece to the system - 8 vocabulary chains that bind the 22 studies into one narrative: (1) kaphar-to-tsadaq progression, (2) tame-to-tahor-to-tsadaq arc, (3) biyn chain (19 occurrences), (4) chazon/mar'eh two-track, (5) tinnaten-to-nitsdaq divine passive frame, (6) sealing-to-unsealing arc, (7) emeth-to-alethinos truth restoration, (8) nitsdaq-to-matsdiqey-to-terountes participation chain - 25 series insights (from dan-8-14-series-insights.md) all applicable to the capstone - 20 transition-anchor verses forming the narrative spine - No external corpus queries — this study synthesizes the series itself

    Key structural finding from prior studies¶

    Dan 9:24 is the ONLY verse in Scripture where DOA process-vocabulary (kaphar) and vindication result-vocabulary (tsedeq) appear together. It is the single linguistic bridge connecting Lev 16 to Dan 8:14. Without it, the two vocabulary systems never meet.
